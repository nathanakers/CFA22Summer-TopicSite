These are some of my interests

  • Beach

    I know this is part of volleyball, but I can be at the beach anytime there’s sun. I grew up in Florida and in Southern California—the beach is part of me. I’m new to Seattle, but I drive out to Golden Gardens more often than anywhere else to catch the sunsets.

  • Volleyball

    I played volleyball starting in high school. I was rejected from the team for two years until I finally made the team in my sophomore year. After that I got really good and played club at a high level. Then in San Francisco I joined a beach volleyball community where we had free clinics every week and some amazing tournaments.

  • DIY Projects

    As long as I can remember I have tried my hand at art and making things. I’m not always great, but when I look back to high school I find that I took woodshop, engineering, and art classes all the time; I built my own table and stools during classes, and my dad still has his “DAD” keychain from when I made that for him in middle school. Lately I’ve been interested in leathermaking.
